The Challenge

The Game Is Growing.
The System Isn’t.

Basketball in Nigeria is full of talent — but held back by structural gaps. We identified four critical areas that need immediate infrastructure.

01

Limited Coverage

Basketball lacks visibility and media presence, slowing its growth in mainstream African culture.

02

Representation

Women remain underrepresented, with limited access to courts, coaching, and participation opportunities.

03

Educational Gap

Many promising players lack the foundational knowledge needed to develop and compete globally.

04

Data Systems

There is no central database tracking players, performance metrics, or scouting opportunities.
