Source Methodology

This database combines publicly available Nigerian men’s basketball information from league reports, club announcements, FIBA records, BAL records, Afrobasket profiles, media reports, and direct roster tracking. Records are assigned confidence levels based on source reliability and recency. Players with incomplete or conflicting information are kept in the database but marked for verification.
